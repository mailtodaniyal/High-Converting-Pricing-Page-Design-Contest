Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

How quickly are announcements published? +

Silver packages are published within 2 hours, Gold within 30 minutes, and Platinum instantly. All packages include simultaneous distribution across our website and 7 social channels.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Yes! You can upgrade at any time and the difference will be prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Contact your account manager for seamless transitions.

What's included in the "Big News Blast" Email? +

Exclusive to Platinum tier, this feature sends your major announcements to our entire investor database of 50,000+ subscribers, ensuring maximum visibility for your most important news.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime? +

No long-term contracts required. You can cancel anytime with no penalties. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions? +

Absolutely! For large organizations or unique requirements, we can create custom packages. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.
